Who We Are

The Undergraduate Research Society (URS) is a cross-disciplinary student organization at The Ohio State University whose purpose is to develop undergraduates into research professionals.

We provide structured resources, mentorship, and a collaborative community to guide students at all stages of their research journey, in initiating, executing, and presenting rigorous scholarly and creative work. Inclusive across all disciplines, we complement and connect OSU's existing research resources with peer mentorship and real-world experience.
